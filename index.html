Traditional Nepali Delights

A journey into the soul of Nepali culture through its vibrant and rich cuisine.

Relish a blend of traditional recipes passed down generations, featuring aromatic spices and flavors inspired by Nepal’s diverse landscapes—from the Himalayas to the lush Terai plains.

Rustic Village Platter

Savor the taste of Nepal’s countryside with every bite.

This platter is a tribute to the simple yet delightful dishes enjoyed in rural Nepal, crafted using fresh, seasonal ingredients and traditional cooking methods.

Classic Dal Bhat

Nepal’s national dish, an everyday staple with extraordinary flavors.

Enjoy a hearty meal of steamed rice, lentil soup, seasonal vegetables, and pickles, prepared with love and care to bring you a taste of home.

Spiced Steamed Momos

Nepal’s favorite dumplings, perfect for every occasion.

Filled with spiced vegetables or juicy meat, served with tangy tomato chutney. These steamed delights bring the warmth of Nepali kitchens to your plate.

Authentic Newari Cuisine

A feast of flavors from the historic Newar community.

Delight in a variety of savory and tangy dishes that reflect the rich culture and heritage of Kathmandu Valley’s Newar people.
